Welcome to treetrav

A revolutionary web application designed to make link organization, sharing, and exploration a seamless and personalized experience. treetrav, currently in its beta version, was founded in 2023 to meet the growing need for a comprehensive link management solution that goes beyond mere bookmarking.


Key Features

Link Organization and Sharing

With treetrav, users can effortlessly save, categorize, and manage their links within customizable folders. This intuitive folder system helps users keep their links organized and easily accessible.

Moreover, treetrav enables users to share entire folders with others, allowing for collaborative curation and seamless information sharing.

Personalized Profiles

Users have the ability to personalize their profiles to match their preferences and privacy needs. Whether it's going private, adding a profile picture, or switching to dark mode for a more comfortable viewing experience, treetrav provides the tools to tailor the platform to individual preferences.

Leaf Creation - Quick HTML Pages

In addition to organizing links, treetrav offers a unique feature called "leafs," which allows users to create quick HTML pages using the popular markdown language. This feature gives users the power to craft simple web pages within the platform, adding a layer of customization to their content.

Discover and Follow

treetrav encourages users to explore new content and connect with others who share similar interests. Users can follow those they find intriguing, enabling them to view the latest links posted by their followed users on their personalized home page.


Our Vision

At treetrav, we envision a future where link management and content curation are effortless and enjoyable experiences. Our mission is to continuously improve and expand our platform, incorporating user feedback to create a tool that enhances productivity, collaboration, and knowledge sharing.

Join us on this exciting journey as we refine and develop treetrav into a leading platform that transforms the way we interact with online content.
