Browser Extension: treetrav Link Saver

Extend the power of treetrav to your browser with our convenient browser extension – the treetrav Link Saver. This extension simplifies the process of saving and sharing links from your browser to treetrav, making link management even more efficient.

Key Features of the Extension:

Post Current Tab to treetrav

With a simple click, users can effortlessly post the link of their current browser tab directly to treetrav. No need to navigate away from your browsing session to save important links.

Post All Tabs in the Current Window

Users can also post links from all tabs open in the current browser window with just a few clicks. Streamline your link organization and share multiple links at once.

Supported Browsers

The treetrav Link Saver extension is currently available on the following browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox

Download the extension for your preferred browser and enhance your link management experience with treetrav!

How to Install the Extension:

  1. Google Chrome:
    • Click here to visit the Chrome Web Store.
    • Click "Add to Chrome" and then "Add extension" to install the treetrav Link Saver.
  2. Mozilla Firefox:
    • Click here to visit the Firefox Add-ons page.
    • Click "Add to Firefox" and then "Add" to install the treetrav Link Saver.

Stay connected and efficiently manage your links across the web using the treetrav Link Saver browser extension. Happy linking!
